Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Who can join this course?
Anyone interested in becoming job-ready! Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to enhance your skills, STRIKE is designed for all levels. Basic computer knowledge and a desire to learn are all you need.
Are there any prerequisites?
No prior coding experience is required. We start from the absolute basics and gradually progress to advanced topics. All you need is dedication and consistency.
Are there projects in this course?
Yes! STRIKE is completely project-based. You'll work on multiple AI-powered projects, full-stack applications, and algorithm implementations. Each project is designed to boost your portfolio and interview prospects.
Is the course live or recorded?
The course features daily live classes Monday to Saturday at 8:30 PM IST. All sessions are recorded and available within 3-4 hours of the live class. You'll also get Aptitude & Reasoning content as pre-recorded videos to start immediately.
What if I have doubts?
Join our exclusive Discord community accessible from your classroom page. Our mentors provide 24/7 support to resolve all your doubts. You can also use the 'Ask Doubt' feature while watching recorded videos.
How long will I have access to the course?
You get full access to the course for 2 years from the date of purchase. During this time, you can revisit lessons, review content, and practice at your own pace. Community access is lifetime!
What if I miss a live session?
Don't worry! All sessions are recorded in high quality and will be available for you to watch anytime. Recordings are uploaded within 3-4 hours of the live class.
How many hours per day are the sessions?
Each live session typically lasts 1.5 hours, but may extend depending on the topic complexity and student questions. We ensure every concept is thoroughly covered.
Who are the instructors?
The course is taught by Harsh Sharma Sir (Founder) and top instructors from Sheryians Coding School, all with extensive industry experience and proven teaching track records.
Do you provide placement assistance?
Yes! We provide comprehensive placement assistance including resume building, mock interviews, LinkedIn optimization, and direct referrals to 50+ partner companies. Our placement rate is 95%.
